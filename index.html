Framecruncher

This is a simple tool for calculating what bitrate you should use when encoding video. This is by no means an exhaustive guide to video encoding and the number it spits out may not be applicable in all situations.

Instructions

  1. Type in your desired file size for the end video file.
  2. Look at the timecode of your video. Let's say for example it's 00:30:20 (30 minutes, 20 seconds).
  3. Type the minutes from that timecode into the "Minutes in video" field
  4. Type the seconds from that timecode into the "Seconds in video" field
  5. Crunch the numbers!
